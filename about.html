About Madsen's Printhouse

Lynn started screen printing for “Spectra Symbol” in Salt Lake City back in 1987. Upon moving to St. George, Lynn was the sole screen printer for many years at Hurst Screen Printing. After getting married, Lynn and Marie decided to open their own screen printing business in 1996. With Marie’s education and background in business and marketing and Lynn’s education in the social sciences, Lynn and Marie have become an effective team, providing many years of quality service to schools, businesses, corporations, religious organizations, friends and relatives. We are excited about our growth each year and attribute this growth to quality customer service. We would like to thank everyone that has employed our skills through these many years and let you know we re-commit ourselves each day to providing you with a great experience as you allow us to “PRINT YOUR MEMORIES.”
